Brown Troat

The brown troat is very tasty especially with some rosemary and thyme.

  • Chromosomes: 38 to 42 pairs
  • Scientific Name: Salmo trutta
  • Lifespan: 10 years
  • Weight: 4 pounds

The brown trout (Salmo trutta) is a European species of salmonid fish that has been widely introduced into suitable environments globally. It includes both purely freshwater populations, referred to as the riverine ecotype or Salmo trutta morpha fario and a lacustrine ecotype, S. trutta morpha lacustris, as well as anadromous forms known as the sea trout, S. trutta morpha trutta.
